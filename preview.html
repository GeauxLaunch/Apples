🍎 All About Apples

What Are Apples?

Apples are sweet, edible fruits produced by apple trees. They come in many colors including red, green, and yellow.

Popular Types

  • Red Delicious
  • Granny Smith
  • Fuji
  • Gala

Fun Facts

Apples float in water because they are 25% air!

There are over 7,500 varieties of apples worldwide.
